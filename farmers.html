Benefits for Farmers and FPOs

Your crop residue has value. We help you earn from it while reducing stubble burning and improving air quality in your village.

What You Can Expect

  • Extra income from crop residue. This is bonus income from material that normally has no market value.
  • Flexible benefit options such as direct payment or barter with biochar or soil enhancers.
  • Cleaner fields and faster preparation for the next crop cycle.
  • Better air quality in your village.
  • Long-term, trust-based partnership.

Types of Biomass We Accept

  • Paddy straw
  • Sugarcane trash or bagasse
  • Groundnut shells
  • Cotton stalks
  • Maize stalks
  • Sunflower waste
  • Coconut shells or husk
  • Any dry crop residue suitable for briquetting

How It Works

  1. Contact us with location and type of residue.
  2. We assess moisture, timelines, and logistics.
  3. We arrange pickup based on volume.
  4. You receive payment or the barter option selected.
