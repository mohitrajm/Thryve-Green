We plan to follow a structured process to maintain quality and safety, including:

  • Checking moisture levels in incoming biomass
  • Monitoring briquette density and size
  • Maintaining clean and organized work areas
  • Training staff on safe machine handling
  • Tracking production performance over time

The facility layout is planned to support future expansion. As we grow, this site can support additional briquette lines, pellet production, biochar experiments, and other waste based products that align with our mission.
