Two problems, one direction. India produces massive amounts of crop residue while industries still rely on coal and firewood.
We are building solutions that connect both sides in a cleaner way. Our focus is on practical, on-the-ground impact that supports farmers, businesses, and the environment.
The challenge

India has a waste problem and an energy problem

Every year, millions of tons of crop residue are burned in the open. At the same time, industries depend heavily on coal and firewood. This increases air pollution, wastes resources, and leaves farmers without alternatives.
